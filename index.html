Can we stimulate people to live more mindfully by playing a game to contribute to a more sustainable world? A small team of sustainable developers & web designers has taken up the challenge to create a beautiful web game that inspires mindfulness.

In the Mindful Meerkats smartphone game, schools join the challenge to become the most mindful school. Every student creates his personal meerkat avatar that reflects his well-being. All students together reflect the well-being of their school colony compared to other schools. Schools receives a weekly game challenge that is connected to real-life activities which have a positive impact on the students and their environment. Students join the challenges in teams and the results are reflected in the avatar & give more options to personalise it.
